Data Management

All your saved pages and notes are stored locally in your browser. Use these tools to backup or restore your memory.

Export Memory

Download all your saved data as a JSON file.

Import Memory

Restore your data from a previously exported JSON file.

Clear All Data

Permanently delete all your saved pages and notes. This cannot be undone.
